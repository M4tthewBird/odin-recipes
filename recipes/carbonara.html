Italian Carbonara

Please

I beg you not to use cream or garlic

Spaghetti Carbonara Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Guanciale
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Pecorino Romano
  • Olive Oil
  • Quality Pepper

Steps

  1. Put Spaghetti into boiling water
  2. Grate 50g of Parmigiano Reggiano and 50g of Pecorino Romano
  3. Separate egg yolks from egg whites
  4. Put four egg yolks into bowl with cheese and mix
  5. Put Guanciale into pan on medium heat and stir until evenly browned
  6. Remove the pan from heat and put the fat from the Guanciale into the bowl with cheese and mix
  7. Add cooked pasta to the pan with Guanciale. Add the mixture from the bowl and mix well on very low heat
  8. Add pepper and salt
  9. On plate add more grated cheese on top and sprink with pepper
