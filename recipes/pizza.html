How to Crunchy Pizza Crust

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim â€“ and it puts the store-bought stuff to shame.

Pizza Crust Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Direction

  1. Gather all ingredients + Preheat oven to 230 Celsius
  2. Place warm water in a bowl; add yeast and sugar. Mix and let stand until creamy, about 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture; beat until smooth.
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle.
  6. Transfer to the prepared pizza pan. + Add toppings of your choice.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
  8. EAT!
